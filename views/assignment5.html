Challenge 5: Accessibility Concerns

Nothing to change but I learned about Form element built-in accessibility

A big accessibility concern of mine related to the challenges would be for Challenge 3: Form. The form element would be one the most common ones seen in regular Internet use, and will often serve a very important role (for instance: Logins, registration forms, purchasing, etc.). In its current state, it is not very accessible to those with vision disability, typing disability, or those lacking writing ability. To rectify these concerns, I think I would add a screen reader, a voice input for hands-off typing and to help those who have difficulty writing.
